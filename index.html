Our Tools

Check out our tools below:

  • Monte Carlo Analysis - this is a valuable tool that assess the probability of your retirement plan's success under various market conditions. It runs thousands of simulations with different market returns, inflation rates, and other variables to project a range of potential retirement outcomes.
  • Home Downpayment Calculator- have you ever wondered how much of a downpayment you'll need to get the monthly mortgage payment you want? If so, you've come to the right place! The home downpayment calculator will determine the amount you'll need to put down on a home to get the monthly payment you want. It also has a rental property feature to determine how much of a downpayment you'll need to place on a rental property to ensure positive cash flow.
  • Credit Score- this tool estimates your credit score and how different variables may impact it.

Please note that this website provides estimates only and should not replace professional advice.
